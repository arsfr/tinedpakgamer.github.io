M Centers primarily focuses on open-source projects related to the identification of vulnerabilities within the Windows operating system, with a specific emphasis on the Universal Windows Platform (UWP) purportedly marketed as a secure platform. The objective of the project is educational in nature, aiming to assist publishers in making informed decisions regarding the adoption of the UWP platform.
